# Privacy Policy for TaxNaija: Nigerian Tax Calculator

**Last Updated: January 15, 2026**

## Introduction
Nigerian Tax Calculator ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

## Disclaimer
TaxNaija: The Nigerian Tax Calculator is not a product or affiliated with the Nigerian Government. All information and calculations within the app are sourced from the publicly available Nigerian Tax Act, 2025 (https://www.nrs.gov.ng/uploads/NIGERIA_TAX_ACT_2025_ef6bb812a5.pdf). 
Users are advised to consult a certified legal or financial adviser before making any financial decision. This app is just a tool to quickly calculate and keep track of your taxable income. 

## Information Collection and Use
Nigerian Tax Calculator is designed with privacy in mind:

### Data Storage
- All tax calculations are stored **locally on your device only**
- We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers
- We do NOT have access to your tax calculations or personal data

### Biometric Data
- If you enable biometric authentication (fingerprint/face ID), this data is processed entirely on your device
- We do NOT collect or store biometric information
- Biometric authentication is handled by your device's operating system

### App Lock PIN
- Your 6-digit PIN is stored securely on your device using encrypted storage
- We do NOT have access to your PIN
- Your PIN never leaves your device

## Permissions
The app requests the following permissions:
- **Biometric/Fingerprint**: For optional biometric app lock feature
- **Storage**: For saving tax calculations locally on your device

## Third-Party Services
This app does NOT use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Security
- All data is stored locally using industry-standard encryption
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No internet connection is required for the app to function

## Children's Privacy
Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for adults who need to calculate taxes.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- You control all your information
